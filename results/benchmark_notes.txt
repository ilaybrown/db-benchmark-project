Benchmark executed from VM1 runner against PostgreSQL and ClickHouse on VM2.

Runner VM: course-group_01-vm1
DB VM: course-group_01-vm2
Warmup runs: 2
Measured runs: 10

Results:
- ClickHouse was faster on all three queries.
- Query 3 showed the largest median speedup: about 7.22x faster.
- Query 2 was also significantly faster on ClickHouse: about 6.06x by median runtime.
- Query 3 had one ClickHouse runtime spike, so median is more representative than mean.
- Row counts matched between PostgreSQL and ClickHouse for every query.
